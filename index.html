About Us

Innovators Hub is a student-run startup community at the University of Washington Bothell that empowers builders across STEM, business, and design to launch real projects with real impact.

We're currently wrapping up our Launchpad S25 Founder Batch, a 5-week incubator program where selected student teams developed startup ideas from scratch with mentorship and peer support from successful entrepreneurs.

Our grand event, Demo Day, is happening soon — and we'd love to have you join us.
